### Competitor Analysis

#### SEOquake

- **Description**: SEMrush-developed extension for SEO metrics
- **Key Features**: SERP overlay, domain metrics, export capabilities
- **Pricing**: Free with premium features
- **Market Position**: Established player, technical users

#### Ubersuggest

- **Description**: Neil Patel's SEO tool with browser integration
- **Key Features**: Keyword suggestions, site audit, content ideas
- **Pricing**: Limited free tier, subscription for full access
- **Market Position**: Growing rapidly, content marketers
